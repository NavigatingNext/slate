Slate’s Core Model Positioning

Who We Aren’t
  • Not a balance‑sheet lender (we don’t fund loans with our own capital)
  • Not a warehouse or debt provider
  • Not the credit decision‑maker (banks & lenders own that risk)
Who We Are
  • Provide integration rails (APIs, dashboards, widgets)
  • Automate compliance & servicing (KYC/AML, collections, reporting)
  • Supply underwriting infrastructure lenders can plug into
  • Let platforms offer lending instantly without building a bank inside
Where We Sit — The Orchestration Layer
